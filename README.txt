In The Dark
-----------
A short interactive top-down game.
A boy wakes up in the dark and his toys turn on him! Help him find his way to his Mum!
Based on theme given by Indie Speed Run - Theme: Childhood, Element: Concert

Lead Programmer: Jon Lew
Programmer: Nicholas Ng
Designer: Lim Yiming
Artist: Malcolm Quah

Controls:
---------
Arrow Keys: Movement
Shift: Run

The boy's torchlight points at the direction that the boy is facing.  Shine them on ghosts to keep them from chasing you.
